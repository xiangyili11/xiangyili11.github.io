GWAS 学习——概念：变异 | 等位基因 | 连锁不平衡

Posted on 2021-01-17 | In GWAS |
Words count in article: 359字

1）variant is used to refer to a specific region of the genome which differs between two genomes. variant
变异，是一个相对概念，是一个区域，相对于另一个个体基因组而言。

2）alleles：Different versions of the same variant are called alleles，For example, a SNP may have two alternative bases, or alleles, C and T 一个变异的不同版本叫做这个变异的 allele。在基因组层面上，参考基因组上的基因就称为 reference allele。对于特定的队列 (cohort）, 最常见的、出现频率最高的等位基因叫做 major allele。出现频率第二多的等位基因叫做 minor allele。参考等位基因（reference allele）之外的等位基因都叫做 alternative allele。

When working with genome scale data the term reference allele refers to the base that is found in the reference genome.
Since the reference is just somebody’s genome, it is not always the major allele.
In contrast, the alternative allele refers to any base, other than the reference, that is found at that locus.
The alternative allele is not necessarily the minor allele and it may, or may not, be linked to a phenotype. There can be more than one alternative allele per variant.

3）连锁不平衡
What is linkage disequilibrium?
In the genome, alleles at variants close together on the same chromosome tend to occur together more often than is expected by chance. These blocks of alleles are called haplotypes. Linkage disequilibrium (LD) is a measure of how often two alleles or specific sequences are inherited together, with alleles that are always co-inherited said to be in linkage disequilibrium.

LD 是一种测量方法，用来计算同一条染色体上两个等位基因一起遗传的频率。
